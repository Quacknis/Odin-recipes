Feijão Tropeiro

Description

A savory dish made with the core of beans and cassava. This dish is incredibly versatile and you may add any other veggies to increase its flavor.

  • 2 cups of beans.
  • 1 onion.
  • 2 diced garlic cloves.
  • 200g of bacon (alternatively you can use rat meat).
  • 200g of smoked dry sausage.
  • 1 cup of shredded cabbage.
  • 1 cup of cassava grains.
  • Oil or rendered fat to taste.
  • Salt and dry seasoning to taste.
  1. First you add the beans into a pressure pan for 30 minutes.
  2. Into a large skillet, begin by adding the sausage and the bacon and stir fry it in 1s tbsp of fat.
  3. Add all your greens and seasonings into it and then stir fry them along with your meat.
  4. Finally add the beans and the cassava grains and mix all until it doesn't feel too wet. You might need to use more cassava.
  5. Serve and eat!
